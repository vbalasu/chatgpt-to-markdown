#### 1. Open your presentation

---

#### 2. Start the slideshow

- Move your mouse (bottom-left corner will appear).

- Find **“Auto-play”**.
- Select **“Every 3 seconds”**.

---

#### 5. Start auto-play

- Click **“Play”**.

### 🔁 Optional: Loop the presentation

If you want it to keep repeating:

- In the same menu, enable **“Loop”**

---

### ⚠️ Important tips

- This only works in **presentation mode**, not edit mode.
- Animations inside slides will also follow the timing—so keep them simple.
- If slides feel rushed, try 5–10 seconds instead.
